Welcome toSchool 1 Campus

School 1 is located in a serene environment, surrounded by lush greenery. Our eco-friendly campus provides an ideal space for students to grow, learn, and connect with nature.

Education is not confined to the classroom. Students engage in nature walks, outdoor classes, and hands-on experiences beneath the trees, promoting a deep respect for the environment. This nature-integrated setting fosters curiosity, mindfulness, and holistic well-being.

Modern Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms equipped with smart boards and digital learning tools to enhance the learning experience.

Science Labs

Fully equipped physics, chemistry, and biology laboratories providing hands-on practical learning opportunities for aspiring scientists.

Library

A vast collection of books, periodicals, and digital resources in our well-stocked library, encouraging a habit of reading and research.

Sports Facilities

Indoor and outdoor sports facilities including a basketball court, football field, and indoor games arena to promote physical fitness.
